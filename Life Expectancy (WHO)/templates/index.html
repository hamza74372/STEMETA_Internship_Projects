About This Predictor

We built a machine learning model to predict life expectancy of countries using the Life Expectancy dataset from the WHO. The dataset includes health, economic, and demographic indicators such as Adult Mortality, Alcohol Consumption, BMI, GDP, Schooling, and Immunization rates.

📊 Simply adjust the sliders below to input values for different health and socioeconomic factors, and our AI model will predict the expected life expectancy based on these indicators.
